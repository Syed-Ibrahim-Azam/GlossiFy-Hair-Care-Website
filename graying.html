Graying Treatment

Causes:
Aging,Genetics,Vitamin Deficiency,Stress,Smoking .

  • 1.Nutritional Supplements
    Taking vitamins like B12, biotin, iron, folic acid, and vitamin D may help if deficiency is a cause. Copper and zinc supplements may support melanin production..
  • 2.Antioxidant-Rich Diet
    Foods high in antioxidants (like berries, green tea, leafy greens) reduce oxidative stress and may delay graying. Omega-3 fatty acids and protein are also beneficial.
  • 3.Topical Treatments
    Products with catalase, melanin precursors, or natural oils (e.g., amla oil, bhringraj) claim to slow down graying. Results vary, and scientific backing is limited.
  • 4.Medical Treatments (Experimental)
    Research into drugs like PC-KUS (a modified pseudocatalase) is ongoing for repigmentation. Stem cell therapy is a potential future option.
  • 5.Hair Dyes (Cosmetic Treatment)
    Most common way to manage graying. Options: Permanent, semi-permanent, and natural dyes (like henna or indigo).
